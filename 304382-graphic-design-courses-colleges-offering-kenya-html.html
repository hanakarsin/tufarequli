What is graphic design?

This can be defined as visual communication and problem-solving technique by the use of illustrations, photography, and formatting. The existing texts or images are developed by a Graphic Designer in traditional or digital form by planning and projecting ideas and concepts into textual or visual concepts. The sole purpose of the course is to influence the viewer’s perception of decoding something.

In Kenya, for instance, they are used in packaging, labelling, and branding of consumables, billboards, signposts, and other advertisements. This is evident in the clothes we wear such as t-shirts with drawings in the form of texts or symbols mainly used to promote and create awareness on matters of health, political or other social campaign projects.

Graphic Design courses

Here are the courses offered in Kenya:

1. Computer-Aided Design Archi-Cad and Autocad

Computer Aided Design commonly referred to as CAD is the use of computer software in facilitating the generation, modification as well as optimisation of a single part or a compilation of different elements. It is mainly used in the entire engineering process starting from conceptualisation, product layout, strength, dynamic analysis and component manufacturing.

2. Cartoon Animation/Rendering

Cartoon Animation is the method of manipulating pictures to make them appear as moving images. Cartoon Animation Rendering teaches the learners on how to automatically generate images that are either photorealistic or non-photorealistic from a 2D or 3D model.

3. Oracle Certifications

Oracle Certification is a course that offers programs that certifies the learners with knowledge and skills relating to Oracles technologies and products.

4. Web Engineering

Web Engineering is the application or systematic, quantifiable and disciplined approaches in the processes of developing, operating and maintaining a web-based application.

5. Software Engineering

In graphic designs, Software Engineering is a course that produces computer science professionals who make use of their skills and knowledge of the principles of engineering and languages of programming to develop computer games, build software products and run network control systems.

6. Computerised Analysis

The course offers the learners with the know-how of applying computer science and critical thinking to aid in the efficient running of businesses. It enables them to design and develop hardware and software systems according to the specifications of the institution involved.

7. Digital Imaging And Motion

Digital Imaging and Motion is a course that offers the knowledge on the ability to capture and produce the image of an object on either a digital display or a film. The generated image can be analysed and reformatted such as seen in ultrasounds, X-Rays and magnetic resonance.

8. Visual Communication

This is the transmission of ideas and information through the use of imagery and symbols. In graphics, the students study illustration, animation, modification, photography, and airbrushing among others.

Graphic Design schools in Kenya

Kenya has a wide range of schools that offer the course either at the certificate, diploma or professional level. One of the key features that make these schools stand out is their mode of teaching. Apart from preparing their learners for the employment world, they also equip them with entrepreneurial knowledge which is vital in starting up their businesses. If you are looking forward to taking one of these courses, be sure to consider one of these graphic’s schools in Kenya.

1. Technical University of Mombasa

The Technical University of Mombasa has been for a long while ranked as the best school for Graphic Design courses. Although its main campus is located in Mombasa, it has successfully opened other branches in the country. At the Technical University of Mombasa, you can study it at certificate, diploma or degree level.

When the name Amboseli Institute of Hospitality and Technology is mentioned, the first thing that comes to the mind is hospitality training.
