Investigators reveal Jacob Juma's movements before he met his death

Publish date: 2024-03-17

- Businessman Jacob Juma was murdered on the night of Thursday, May 5, near Lenana School, according to police officers

- His family has been demanding investigations into his assassination and now CID boss has revealed details of the deceased's movement before he met his death

- Juma was apparently with a lady whom investigators have quizzed. However, the lady says she left him early as he went about his duties

- Juma made movements across the city before meeting his untimely death

READ ALSO: Stop linking police to Jacob Juma's murder, politicians warned

Police officers investigating the cold blood murder of Nairobi businessman Jacob Juma have revealed the last moments he had as captured in cameras across the city.

Giving an update on Tuesday, May 17, Criminal Investigations Department boss Ndegwa Muhoro revealed that investigators have already interrogated a lady seen with the deceased moments prior to his assassination.

Muhoro also detailed some of the roads and places Juma passed and visited before he met his untimely death.

The lady who was accompanying Juma before he was killed admitted she was with him on the material day and had agreed to meet later in the evening.

She says she was later dropped at Rose apartments in Brookside Drive by taxi as they had parted with the deceased.

Here is the breakdown of Juma’s last moments before he was killed as covered by Star:

19.34: the deceased's vehicle is captured opposite Westlands Bus Terminus driving along Waiyaki Way towards the CBD with the deceased on the driver's seat and the passenger.

19.36: the late Juma is captured by the CCTV footage along Waiyaki way near Villa Rosa driving towards Uhuru highway.

19.38: he is captured at Kenyatta/Uhuru highway near Ministry of Tourism, driving along Uhuru highway towards Mombasa road.

19.53: Juma is seen at Uhuru highway near Central park, heading towards Waiyaki Way.

19.55: the deceased is captured at Nyerere Road near Children's Traffic Park, along Nyerere Road towards Kenyatta Avenue.

20.14: the cameras capture Juma at Argwings Kodhek Road, near Yaya Centre, heading towards Ole Odume Road.

According to Muhoro, this was the last footage found on the deceased’s movements.

The latest comes after the deceased’s family demanded detailed investigations into the murder of a man who was deemed controversial considering how vocal he was on social media.

Until his death, Juma was a trong Jubilee government critic and a Raila Odinga supporter. The opposition has claimed that the government had a hand in the murder of the businessman because of the information he allegedly had on the NYS and Eurobond scandals.

Early investigations by police indicated that he was shot twice on the chest after the assassins broke the window of his bullet proof car.

Juma was killed on the night of May 5, 2016, along Ngong Road near Lenana School, police claimed. However, it is suspected he was killed elsewhere and his body and car dumped at the scene.
