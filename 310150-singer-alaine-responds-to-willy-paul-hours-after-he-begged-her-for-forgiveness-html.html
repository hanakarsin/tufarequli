Jamaican singer Alaine responds to Willy Paul hours after he begged her for forgiveness

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Melodious Jamaican songstress Alaine Laughton has responded to gospel singer Willy Paul hours after the musician took to social media to make a humble appeal to her.

According to an earlier report filed by TUKO.co.ke, Willy Paul on Wednesday, July 10, took to social media to complain about how the songstress had blocked him online and has been ignoring him.

The star begged Alaine for forgiveness and narrated how her 'negligence' on their friendship had affected him.

In his post, Pozee asked Alaine not to be mad at him because of singer Nandy as they were only friend and not lovers.

In essence, the gospel crooner was rooting for a romantic re-union as he insinuated the two had a thing, not just music only.

Pozee even pleaded with his fans and followers to help him get the message to Alaine, which they did.

She duly responded barely hours after the Kitanzi hitmaker dragged her into his 'shenanigans'.

In a quick rejoinder on Thursday, July 11, Alaine assured Pozee that everything was going to be fine between them and promised to avail herself for yet another music collabo should he allow it.

The Jamaican beauty kept it professional and stuck to music as she did not mention anything to do with the blocking and not picking his calls.

''Willy, Willy, Willy everything will be alright. It is all about the music, and specifically, all about God. Thanking Him for all His blessings, despite obstacles, despite enemies, in spite of ourselves. We know He has a plan for us to prosper us and to give us a future with hope. We know how far He’s taken us and trust Him with the future. One thing is for sure. We have great musical chemistry, so what do you think one more song? Let’s unblock some blessings,'' she wrote.

Her response got to Willy Paul who was not that impressed as he expected more.

The musician expressed gratitude for the response but wondered why Alaine focused on music instead of talking about 'their' love.

Taking to Instagram, Willy wrote;

''Thank you guys for helping me deliver my message to Alaine. She responded but not what I wanted to hear, but I'm glad I have been unblocked and we are now good, thank you. Now she is bringing stories about music instead of talking about our relationship, anyway, it is all good. Would you love to see us do another one?'' he posed.
