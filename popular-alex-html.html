Popular Alex - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-26

Short-form dance and lip-sync content creator who is best known for sharing performances on her former self-titled TikTok account. She built a fan base of more than 1.1 million followers. She initially gained fame for a video that features the caption "when I get called the word mamas."

Before Fame

Her oldest archived TikTok was posted in March of 2019. She lip-synced to lines from Riverdale in an early video.

Trivia

She shares selfie photos and uplifting messages on her Instagram. She enjoys being herself and showing her confidence. She is part of the LGBTQ+ community and identifies as bisexual. She once posted a video saying various words in a Jamaican accent. 

Family Life

She was born in the United States and is of Jamaican descent. She has been openly bisexual.

Associated With

In September of 2020, she created a dance duet featuring Charli D'Amelio.
