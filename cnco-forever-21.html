CNCO x Forever 21 Collection What to Shop Now!

Publish date: 2024-06-22

If You Love CNCO, You'll Love Their New Forever 21 Collection

By Anna Quintana

Nov. 13 2019, Updated 2:37 p.m. ET

CNCO might sing about a reggaeton lento, but you'll need to be quick if you want to snag a piece from the Latin group's Forever 21 collection. 

The ready-to-wear pieces include everything from graphic tees to bodysuits and edgy jackets for men and women — and come in junior and plus sizes. The collection ranges in price from $4.99 to $34.99 and will be available in over 250 Forever 21 stores in the U.S., Latin America, and Asia.

There is also a CNCO x Forever 21 giveaway. 

If you want one of the pieces the CNCO guys —  Joel Pimentel, Richard Camacho, Erick Brian Colón, Christopher Vélez, and Zabdiel De Jesús — are wearing in the campaign, you're in luck. Those items are all one of a kind and signed by the group, and one lucky fan will be able to win through a social giveaway on Forever 21. 

"We’re so excited to announce our collaboration with @forever21 available now!!!” the group wrote on Instagram. “We are super grateful to be able to have this collaboration and our own line! We hope you guys like it!”

And fans are definitely loving the new merch. 

"Felicidades on this brilliant collection...I am excited for mine to come," one fan tweeted before another added, "CNCO launched a collection with Forever 21 and I'm freaking out because I want to buy everything." 

A third chimed in, "Me walking into forever 21 and spotting the CNCO collection immediately." 

You can wear the merch to see CNCO on tour in 2020!

This year has been great for CNCO. They kicked off 2019 with their debut Spanglish single "Pretend" and they have been keeping busy with their CNCO World Tour. 

More recently, the group released their highly anticipated EP Que Quiénes Somos — and if you haven't listened to "Pegao" you are seriously missing out. 

CNCO has definitely come a long way since their debut in 2015 on La Banda. 

If you want to still catch them on tour, you can. They will be joining Sebastián Yatra in concert in El Paso on Dec. 13. On Dec. 22, CNCO will join French Montana and the Jonas Brothers on the stage in Sunrise, Florida and in March 2020 they will be headlining a show in Monterrey, Mexico. 

BRB, booking our flight now. 

In the meantime, make sure you head over to Forever 21 to cop that CNCO swag before it sells out!
