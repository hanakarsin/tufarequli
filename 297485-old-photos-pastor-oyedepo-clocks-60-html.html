Old Photos Of Bishop Oyedepo As He Clocks 60

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Fondly called 'papa' by many, David O. Oyedepo is the presiding Bishop of Living Faith Church World Wide, also known as Winners' Chapel.

The pastor, who is referred to as one of the most powerful pastor both home and abroad was born 60 years ago today (27th September, 1954). The man of God who was listed as the richest pastor in Nigeria by Forbes in 2011.

READ MORE: The Reason I Fought With Oyedepo And Tore His Book – Pastor Tunde Bakare

However, to celebrate the Diamond Jubilee (60years) of the great man of God, the Nation reports that President Goodluck Jonathan has wished the Presiding Bishop of the Living Faith Church Worldwide, for being a good example to many Nigerians amongst others.

In the letter congratulating Bishop Oyedepo  on his 60th birthday anniversary today, Saturday, September 27, 2014, President Jonathan declared that Bishop Oyedepo’s life was “truly worthy of celebration”, this he made known through his Special Adviser on Media and Publicity, Dr. Reuben Abati.

“I write on behalf of my family, the Government and people of the Federal Republic of Nigeria to rejoice with you as you mark your milestone 60th birthday anniversary.”

“Yours is, indeed, a life truly worthy of celebration. You have attained lofty heights not just as an accomplished selfless servant of God, but also in the committed service of community, country, and our common humanity.”

“You have not only committed to giving your all in pursuit of the massively impactful propagation of the Word, you have also embraced the cause of providing quality education for our children.”

“As Almighty God continues to bless you with robust health and many more years of happiness, uncommon accomplishment, and fulfilling service, it is my prayer that you will continue to partner with us in our quest to reposition Nigeria for peace, progress and prosperity,” Jonathan stated.
