Xenia Deli - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Former Victoria's Secret model whose fame skyrocketed after starring in the Justin Bieber music video for his song "What Do You Mean?"

Before Fame

She first got started in modeling when she was twenty years old. She graduated with a degree in foreign literature.

Trivia

She has graced the covers of both South African and Spanish FHM as well as South African Sports Illustrated.

Family Life

Her sister Vera is a model as well. She is from Basarabeasca, Tighina, Moldova. She married Ossama Fathi Rabah Al-Sharif in 2016; he is over 25 years her senior. She gave birth to her daughter Anastasia in 2018.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Mindy Kaling in a 2013 episode of The Mindy Project.
