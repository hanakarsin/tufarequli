Jill Duggar Dillard - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-21

Best known for starring on the TLC reality series 19 Kids and Counting from 2008 to 2015, she later went on to star on the spin-off series Jill And Jessa: Counting On.

Before Fame

In the season premier of the show, she traveled with her family to New York City to be featured on The Today Show.

Trivia

She celebrated both her 19th and 21st birthday on the show.

Family Life

She was the fourth born in the Duggar family. She married Derick Dillard on June 21, 2014; they have sons named Israel and Samuel. On February 28th, they announced that they were expecting their third child together. She gave birth to their son Freddy in July 2022.

Associated With

Her father is Jim Bob Duggar and her mother is Michelle Duggar.
