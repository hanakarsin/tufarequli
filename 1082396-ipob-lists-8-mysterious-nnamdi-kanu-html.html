IPOB lists 8 mysterious things about Nnamdi Kanu

Publish date: 2024-02-10

- The Indigenous People of Biafra (IPOB) has said that the existence of its leader Nnamdi Kanu is mysterious.

- The group said its agitation for the freedom of Biafra cannot be a fraud as claimed by many due the mystery behind its leader.

- The IPOB leader who was recently granted bail has been released having met his bail conditions

“Look at this this ancient map of Biafra and marvel at the divine force behind Kanu and IPOB,” Emma said.
