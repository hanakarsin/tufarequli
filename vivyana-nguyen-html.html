Vivyana Nguyen - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-14

Social media star who often appears in her family's prank, vlog, and challenge YouTube videos. Their Life With The Nguyen's YouTube channel has accumulated over 310,000 subscribers. She is featured on her vivyana_nguyen Instagram account, run by her mother, where she shares outfits. 

Before Fame

She launched her personal YouTube channel Vivyana's World in March 2020. 

Trivia

One of the family's most popular YouTube videos features her mom reacting to her TikTok videos. They have gained over 70 million views as a family on YouTube.

Family Life

She and her younger brother Benny posted a Roblox video on her personal YouTube channel. She has a sister named Analeigha and her mother's name is Nessa. 

Associated With

She and Elise Johnston are both members of their families' YouTube channels.
