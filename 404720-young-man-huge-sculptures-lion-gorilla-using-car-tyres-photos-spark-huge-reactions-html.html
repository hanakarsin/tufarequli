Young man makes huge sculptures of lion and gorilla using car tyres, photos spark huge reactions

Publish date: 2024-05-09

- Social media users have expressed surprise at a rare display of artistic talent

- This is as photos emerged of a young man who created amazing sculptures of a gorilla and a lion from old car tyres

- Many have adjudged his work as impressive and his talent as amazing

The man, whose identity is still unknown, got many social media users blown away with his uncommon talent.

He created sculptures of gorilla, lion and other wild animals using car tyre waste.

Sharing photos of the man's works on Instagram, African Heroes Magazine described his creations as impressive.

Sharing the images, a Twitter user @Hangwela wrote:

"Some people are talented."

In the images, the man whose identity is still unknown - started with making cracks on a rock with what appears to be an axe.

The second frame showed the work in its semi-finished state with a clearer view of a seated woman taking a view of her surroundings.

In the concluding frames, he put finishing touches and presented a mind-blowing sculpture.
