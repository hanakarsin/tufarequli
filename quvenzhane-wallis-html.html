Quvenzhane Wallis - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-16

Had her breakthrough role as Hushpuppy in the 2012 indie film Beasts of the Southern Wild, which earned her an Academy Award nomination. She also received a Golden Globe nomination for Best Actress in a Motion Picture, Musical or Comedy for her role in the 2014 film Annie. 

Before Fame

She lied about her age when auditioning for Beasts of the Southern Wild. 

Trivia

She appeared in the Best Picture winner 12 Years a Slave with Michael Fassbender. 

Family Life

She has three siblings. Her mother is Qulyndreia and her father is Venjie Wallis, Sr. 

Associated With

She starred in Beasts of the Southern Wild, which was directed by Benh Zeitlin.
