Preachers wife dies from snake bite on their wedding night

Publish date: 2024-03-15

A Nigerian man has been left mourning just a day after his wedding following the sudden death of his wife.

The founder of Miracle Working God Ministry, Patrick Eyaefe, revealed his lovely bride lost her life after succumbing to a snake bite on their wedding night.

According to Ghanaweb.com, the couple was together for eight years but decided to walk down the aisle in April 2019.

The duo had five children together.

According to the 44-year-old self-proclaimed prophet, his wife left the house to run an errand at a local shop.

“She had barely left home for five minutes when I heard her screaming for help. We were relaxing in the evening around 7pm after the wedding when she went out,” the prophet said.

While relaxing at home, Patrick heard a sharp scream from outside.

After hearing the scream, he rushed outside and found his wife wailing. She told him she had been bitten by a snake.

The ailing lady was rushed to hospital but sadly, she could not be treated in time.

“She died leaving five kids for me. How do I go on?” he cried.
