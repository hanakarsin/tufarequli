Yul Edochie Reacts As He Gets Support From Northern Youth Group

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Nollywood actor and 2023 presidential aspirant, Yul Edochie has commended the North Central Youths for pledging  their support for his Presidential ambition.

The actor took to his verified Instagram page to share the support poster and appreciating the North Central youths for their support and unity.

The 39-year-old actor, further stated that it is indeed the time for the youths to take over the affairs of the great nation, Nigeria.

According to him. the older generation divided and crippled the country for too long with Tribalism and religion, noting that it is time fo unity and moving the country forward.

Sharing the support poster from the North Youth Central, he wrote, ” Thank you to my North Central brothers and sisters for the support. The older generation have divided and crippled us for too long with Tribalism and Religion. We’re tired of it. It’s time to unite and move our country forward. We’re one. Yes we can and we will.”

Recall, the Popular Nigerian actor, Yul Edochie recently revealed why the former presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Atiku Abubakar should support his presidential ambition.

According to Edochie, Atiku should support him in becoming Nigeria’s president in 2023, the same way he did for him in 2019.

Edochie in a post on his Instagram account said, “I did it with my full chest, proudly. I did not do it for money. I was not paid to do it. May God punish me if I lie.

“I even resigned from my Government appointment then so I could give Atiku my full support without any hindrance.”
