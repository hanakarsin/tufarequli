Man Breaks Up with Girlfriend Months after Saying He Loves Her More than His Mother

Publish date: 2024-06-08
  • Lovebirds who hooked up on Ghana's reality show Date Rush Shemima and Ali have parted ways
  • The voluptuous actress revealed that Ali was already dating before joining the reality show
  • Shemima announced that they were no longer together in a video she posted on Instagram
  • She recounted how a certain lady kept threatening her to leave Ali and find her own man
  • Shemima said Ali did not value the love and ended up breaking her heart, leaving her disheartened

Shemima of Date Rush reality show has revealed that she has broken up with her boyfriend Ali, who she met on the same show.

While speaking in a self-recorded video, Shemima wept uncontrollably as she officially announced her separation from Ali.

According to her, Ali started showing disinterest during the first stages of their relationship and pushed them apart, leading to the breakup.

Shemima said she turned down many suitors because of Ali and did so because she loved him. However, she said Ali did not value the love and ended up breaking her heart, leaving her disheartened.

Shemima went on to add that she had regretted going on national television to look for love and announced she was now very single and moving on with her life.

Among many other reasons that led to the breakup, Shemima recounted that she was always at the receiving end of warnings and threats from a certain lady asking her to stay away from Ali.

She discovered that Ali was already dating before joining the show to look for a soulmate.
