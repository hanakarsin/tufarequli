Revered country music star Sir Elvis bags prestigious award in Texas, US

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Kenya continues to create ripples, with the latest being winning a worldwide accolade in country music.

This, after country music crooner Sir Elvis won an international award in the genre.

The musician recently made himself and Kenya proud after bagging an award during the just concluded Texas Sounds Awards held in Texas, USA.

The talented country music star, one of the few in the country, beat his competition to clinch the Best Male Vocalist of the year award.

The musician took to social media to thank his Kenyan fans for always rallying behind him as he displayed the award.

''I am so excited and very humbled to announce that the award for the Best Male vocalist of the year is home. Thank you everyone for the love and support. You played a major role in making this dream come true. We just got started, expect more of this,'' he wrote on Instagram.

Kenyans congratulated him and wished him well following the feat.
