Shelby Chesnes - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-12

Named Playmate of the Month for July of 2012, she is a model and actress with film credits in Last Vegas and Horrible Bosses 2.

Before Fame

She studied psychology at Florida Atlantic University, while she also ran a successful spray tan business. Before being named Miss July 2012, she posed for a local sunglass company and car ads.

Trivia

She was named a "social star" by Playboy Magazine in 2014 for her social media followings on Twitter, Instagram and Facebook.

Family Life

She was born in Jupiter, Florida. Her mother was an avid photographer.

Associated With

She was featured in the Jason Bateman starring film Horrible Bosses 2.
