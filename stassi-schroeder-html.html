Stassi Schroeder - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-12

American television personality and model who appeared on the reality series Vanderpump Rules. She also appeared on The Amazing Race.

Before Fame

She started taking theatre classes at her high school and attended Loyola Marymount University.

Trivia

She has also worked as a fashion columnist for The Divine Addiction.

Family Life

She has a younger brother named Hunter. In September 2020, she wed her Vanderpump Rules co-star Beau Clark. She and Clark welcomed their first child, Hartford Charlie Rose, in January 2021. She previously dated Patrick Meagher and another Vanderpump Rules personality, Peter Madrigal.

Associated With

She appeared on the reality show Vanderpump Rules like Scheana Marie.
