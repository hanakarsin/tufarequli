Heather Gay - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-21

Reality television star best known for being a cast member of the show The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City. She was raised Mormon and was devoted to the religion for years before breaking away from it. 

Before Fame

She joined the Real Housewives cast in November 2020. 

Trivia

She owns a Med-Spa business called Beauty Lab and Laser, where many of the housewives can be seen frequenting. 

Family Life

She was married to a man whose identity has been kept private for 11 years before they divorced. She has three daughters named Ashley, Georgia, and Annabelle. 

Associated With

She appears alongside Jen Shah on The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City.
