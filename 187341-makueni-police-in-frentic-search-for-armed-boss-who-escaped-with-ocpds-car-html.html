Police in pursuit of OCS on the run with boss's vehicle

Publish date: 2024-02-15

- Police have launched a manhunt for an OCS who escaped with a vehicle belonging to his boss

- The Officer, named Abraham Borborei ,went missing with the OCPD's official vehicle and is said to be armed

The Officer Commanding Police Division (OCPD) Mbooni, James Baraza has sent a security alert after a senior police officer escaped with his vehicle.

The OCPD, in the alert, said Abraham Borborei -the Officer Commanding Station (OCS) for Mbooni west- disappeared with his official vehicle.
