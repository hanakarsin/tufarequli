Mike Sonko's youngest daughter Sandra consoles dad as he lies in hospital bed

Publish date: 2024-06-13

- Sandra has been her father's pillar of strength ever since his woes started piling up

- She visited him while he was locked up and made sure the former governor felt loved and appreciated

- Recently, the young lady shared a photo of her dad lying in a hospital bed and left a message of encouragement for him

For nearly two weeks, former Nairobi Governor Mike Sonko has felt like his world turned upside down.

He has felt disgraced, frustrated and betrayed thanks to his short stay behind bars.

His youngest daughter Sandra has been his rock through it all and has joined her mother and sisters whenever they wanted to either visit Sonko or uplift him.

The youngster had to become an adult in less than 14 days just so her Papa would not feel the weight of the world on his shoulders.

Sandra posted a photo of the ousted governor lying in a hospital bed looking demotivated and immensely tired.

The formerly vocal politician was humbled, quiet, covered in hospital clothing and appeared to be too weak.

Sandra stood by daddy’s side and assured him that this too shall pass.

"They throw us in the wolves, we come back leading the pack. Love you papa,” Sandra declared.

After watching her father go through hellfire, Sonko’s youngest daughter Sandra decided to speak up.

But she kept it short, sweet and hopeful to avoid delving too deep into the matter.

First, the young lady shared a tweet that alluded to the fact that her wealthy dad was only being used as a pawn.

It further painted the disgruntled politician as the victim of a grander scheme concocted to destroy him.
