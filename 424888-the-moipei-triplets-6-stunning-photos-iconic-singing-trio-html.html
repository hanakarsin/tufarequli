Publish date: 2024-08-05
  • Everyone remembers the Moipei triplets for their sterling performance during President Uhuru Kenyatta's inauguration in 2013
  • The group has become very popular in Kenya, gaining accolades for their splendid performances.
  • In 2015, the sisters became the first non-Americans to perform the Star Spangled Banner at NBA Playoff Game

Kenya's singing beauties, Mary, Marta and Magdaline, are the Moipei triplets that took the Kenyan music industry by storm in 2013.

The three gave a splendid performance during President Uhuru Kenyatta's inauguration.

TUKO.co.ke highlights six photos of the beautiful trio that graduated with first-class honours at St Mary’s University in Antonio, Texas, USA on Sunday, May 15 2015.
