Daily Dose of Internet - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-24

YouTuber named Jason and also known as Nosaj who runs the YouTube channel Daily Dose of Internet, which aggregates the latest viral videos. His channel has posted viral hits like "Voice Actor of Spongebob Swearing" and "Throwing Bananas at People That Text and Drive." He has also posted many videos that include animal and science related content. 

Before Fame

He grew up in Chicago, Illinois and is a big fan of the Chicago White Sox. 

Trivia

He has a separate gaming channel called Sir Nosaj, where he posts footage from game franchises like Grand Theft Auto and Call of Duty. A compilation video he posted at the end of 2019 showed his favorite internet clips from that year and has received over 46 million views. 

Family Life

He has an older sister. 

Associated With

One of his most popular videos is "Bill Nye in Elevator With Half Naked Women."
