The world was thrown into great mourning as the popular prophet and overseer of Synagogue Church of all Nations (SCOAN), TB Joshua, died in the early hours of Sunday, June 6.

Tributes and praises of the SCOAN prophet took over social media platforms as people spoke about his numerous acts of generosity.

On the same Sunday, a Facebook user and follower of the late man of God, Prince Toyin Akingbade, shared throwback photos of the prophet that further confirm what people have been saying about him.

He fed many people

In one of the snaps, the humble prophet carried three bags of food on his head, sweating in his shirt. Another has him giving out foodstuff in abundance to people who needed it.

In a photograph, he took it upon himself to keep the church clean as he got to work wiping the chairs of dirt and dust.
