October 26 Horoscope and Zodiac

Publish date: 2024-03-15

Your sign's ruling planet is Pluto and as you were born in the first Decan, or part, of the sign, Pluto's influence is twice as strong within you. Pluto is the planet of transformation, power and regeneration, which explains why you strive for these things in life. You wont be satisfied until you reach the level of success that you desire, which makes you ever-ambitious, especially in financial matters. Although your ambition is one of your greatest assets, be careful to avoid abusing your power. If you begin to rely too heavily on manipulation and selfish ambition, your personal relationships will suffer.
