Anetra - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-25

American drag performer who is known for being a contestant on the fifteenth season of RuPaul's Drag Race. Her Instagram account iamanetra has gained over 600,000 followers. She posts runway and fashion content on the popular account. 

Before Fame

In April 2018, she debuted as a drag queen at the CSN Cheyenne Campus Horn Theater.

Trivia

She chose the name "Anetra" because that was the name of a stripper that she used to work with and mentioned she was "the hottest thing she ever saw in her life". 

Family Life

She used to belong to the same drag family as India Ferrah. 

Associated With

She and Sasha Colby both competed on RuPaul's Drag Race.
