Portable's Wife Fumes As He Set To Marry Pregnant Lover

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Nigerian singer, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as portable, is set to marry his pregnant lover, identified as Keji.

Naija News reports that the controversial singer made headlines earlier this week for impregnating another woman and bringing her into his matrimonial home to live with his wife, Bewaji.

Bewaji who already has two children with Portable reportedly got angry, but she was pacified by family members, and she agreed to accept the situation.

However, a source who spoke with PUNCH in an interview confirmed the pregnancy speculations, stating that Potable plans to marry Keji.

The controversial singer met the lady eight months ago at a birthday party in the Agege area of Lagos State and he has finalized plans to marry her.

According to the source, Keji was staying with Portable’s manager, Steliza before she moved into the singer’s home.

The source added that Bewaji was angry with the decision considering she has been with the singer before he came to the limelight.

The source said: “The name of the girl is ‘Keji’, and he has been dating her for about eight months. They met between January and February at the party of a guy nicknamed, ‘Ogo Hookup’, in Agege, Lagos.

“He actually plans to marry her.

“The girl is the fourth girl in his life. He also has another lady called Asabi, and she is pregnant as well.

“Bewaji got angry because she had been with Portable before he became popular.”
