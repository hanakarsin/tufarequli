Lauren Londons Kids: Meet the Actress's Children

Publish date: 2024-03-06

From her previous relationships with Lil Wayne and Nipsey Hussle, Lauren London has two sons named Kameron and Kross. Details ahead.

The Gist:

  • Actress Lauren London rose to fame starring in music videos before hitting it big in film and television.
  • She's also a proud mother to two sons, one of whom she shares with Lil Wayne, and the other of whom she welcomed with the late Nipsey Hussle.

Article continues below advertisement

Actress Lauren London rose to fame starring in music videos with artists Tyrese, Pharrell, Ludacris, and Snoop Dogg, before hitting it big with television shows and feature films such as ATL, This Christmas, Baggage Claim, and Netflix’s You People.

Aside from her filmography, Lauren is also known for her high-profile relationships with rapper Lil Wayne and the late Nipsey Hussle, who was fatally shot in front of his L.A.-based clothing store in 2019. From each relationship, Lauren has a son, both of whom serve as a source of inspiration and purpose for the actress. Keep reading to learn more about her kids.

Article continues below advertisement

Who are Lauren London’s kids?

Lauren shares her son Kameron (born in 2009) with Lil Wayne, and from her relationship with Nipsey Hussle she has a son named Kross (born in 2016).

Working to maintain her family’s privacy, Lauren only shares photos of her sons occasionally, but when she does, they come with heartfelt messages of loving her children and the journey of motherhood. She affectionately refers to her boys as King Kam and Kross the Boss.

Article continues below advertisement

Kameron Carter, Lauren’s eldest son, was born on Sept. 9, 2009, while Lauren and Lil Wayne were still together. Lauren met Lil Wayne when she was 15 years old, and their relationship over the years had many ups and downs, including an engagement, a breakup, and an attempt to rekindle what was.

In the end, Lauren realized they were better off as friends, and she has nothing but positive things to say about Lil Wayne: “My son’s father is an intelligent, loving, and lovable person who will always be a dear friend. And that will never change.”

Lauren gave birth to Kross Ermias Asghedom on Aug. 31, 2016, and at the time, she was in a long-term relationship with rapper Nipsey Hussle. The couple was together from 2013 until 2019 when Hussle was murdered. To this day, Lauren deals with the grief of her loss and openly shares her feelings with her sons to create an open and trusting bond between the three of them.

“It’s important for my sons to see me moving forward with grief, not just curling up in a ball because I curled up in a ball for a long time,” Lauren told Entertainment Tonight last spring.

The mother of two puts her sons first and her career second, but that doesn’t mean Lauren hasn’t been busy in front of the camera.

Since losing her partner, Lauren has kept a low profile, focusing on healing and raising her two sons. She’s starred in a handful of television shows and movies, including 2023's You People alongside Jonah Hill. And in fall 2023, she released a collection with Puma called Protect Your Peace.

As Puma's site describes it: "This new collection is centered around a principle [Lauren] lives her life by, 'protect your peace.' To Lauren, this means cultivating inner peace despite outer circumstances. Apparel and footwear pieces are centered around sayings and symbols that bring inner peace and balance."

Who is Lauren London dating?

It's unclear if Lauren is seeing anyone at the moment. She appears to be focusing on her family while also continuing with her career.

Lauren's known dating history primarily consists of her aforementioned relationships with Lil Wayne and Nipsey Hussle. Amid her on-and-off time with Lil Wayne, though, she was linked to rapper Neef Buck and professional skateboarder Terry Kennedy.

After her relationship with Lil Wayne ended, there were rumors that she was with Trey Songz. She denied being together with Trey, though Trey claimed that they had indeed dated.
