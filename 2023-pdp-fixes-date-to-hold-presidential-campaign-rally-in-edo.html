PDP Fixes Date To Hold Presidential Campaign Rally In Edo

Publish date: 2024-06-21

The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) is set hold its presidential campaign rally on Saturday, October 22, in Benin, Edo State capital.

Naija News recalls the major opposition party had launched its presidential campaign rally in Uyo, the Akwa Ibom State capital two weeks ago, and also held another rally in Kaduna State last week.

In a statement Thursday, Chairman of the PDP Presidential Campaign Organization and Sokoto State Governor, Aminu Tambuwal, said the rally would hold at the Samuel Ogbemudia Stadium in Benin City, by 10am.

Tambuwam charged all party leaders and supporters to make their way to the Edo state capital and join hands with the party’s Presidential Candidate, Atiku Abubakar and the Vice presidential candidate, Ifeanyi Okowa to rescue Nigeria.

The statement read in part “This invite all elected PDP Governors, members of  PDP National Working Committee, NASS Members, NEC members, BOT, members, state chapters, former governors, former Ministers, PDP candidates ; and stakeholders to the EDO State presidential campaign rally as scheduled below:

“Date: Saturday 22nd October, 2022, the venue is Samuel Ogbemudia Stadium, Benin City, Edo State and the time is 10:00AM prompt

“Please endeavour to attend and participate as we join hands with our Presidential Candidate, H.E. Atiku Abubakar, GCON, (Waziri Adamawa) and Vice presidential candidate, H. E Senator Dr Ifeanyi Okowa CON, to Rescue Nigeria”.
