In Love With A Ghost - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Producer who built a loyal fanbase as an independent artist using platforms like SoundCloud and YouTube, where they typically post low-key, keyboard-driven electronic music. Some of their most popular tracks include "Flowers," "I Thought We Were Lovers" and "We've Never Met But, Can We Have a Coffee or Something?"

Before Fame

They grew up in the Paris area of France and began posting music in 2013.

Trivia

Their 2016 YouTube video for "We've Never Met But, Can We Have a Coffee or Something?" has been viewed more than 5 million times.

Family Life

They have a sister.

Associated With

Their song "Complex Feelings About My Own Existence" features Jungkook of Bangtan Boys fame.
