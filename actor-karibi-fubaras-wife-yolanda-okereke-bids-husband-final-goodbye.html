Actor Karibi Fubaras Wife, Yolanda Okereke Bids Husband Final Goodbye

Publish date: 2024-06-05

The wife of Actor Karibi Fubara, Olanda Okereke, has taken to social media to bid her husband a final farewell as he is finally laid to rest today, 11th February 2022.

Naija News recalls that Nollywood actor, Karibi Fubara died after a long battle with cancer.

Taking to her Instagram page, Olanda shared their wedding photo whilst urging her husband to soar with the angels.

 “S. O. A. R. W. I. T. H. T. H. E. A. N. G. E. L. S. Bara And Bambi Hes Alive” She wrote.

Meanwhile, veteran Nollywood actor, Romanus Amuta popularly known for playing Natty in New Masquerade has died.

Naija News reports that, Chukwuma Amuta, the deceased son stated that the veteran died in a hospital on February 9 after a protracted illness.

“Yes! My father died this morning in Lagos after a protracted illness.”

Amuta was an indigene of ukana in Udi LGA of Enugu. He worked with Daily Times before venturing into the movie industry. The actor came into the lime light after he featured in the movie ‘New Masquerade.’
