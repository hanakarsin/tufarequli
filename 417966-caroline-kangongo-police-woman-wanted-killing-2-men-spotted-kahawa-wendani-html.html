Caroline Kangogo, a rogue police officer, described as armed and dangerous by the Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI), has been spotted in Kahawa Wendani.

The DCI detectives received information that indicated Kangogo was seen around Celvet Club in Kahawa Wendani at around 2 am on Wednesday, July 7.

The police corporal was then spotted hopping on a motorbike heading towards Nairobi through Thika Road before exiting at Drive Inn.

The detectives said Kangogo is a person of interest in the murder of police constable John Ogweno in Nakuru on Monday, July 4.

The officer then took off with Ogweno’s firearm, a loaded Ceska pistol and drove to Juja in Kiambu county, where she lured another man to Dedamax Hotel on Monday, July 5.

She later emerged from the room at around midnight and fled to an unknown destination, leaving the dead man’s body sprawled on the bed.

Detectives established that she lured her second victim to the room after paying for it before savagely shooting him on the head at point-blank.

She later told an attendant working at the guest house that she had gone to buy toothpaste before making her escape.
