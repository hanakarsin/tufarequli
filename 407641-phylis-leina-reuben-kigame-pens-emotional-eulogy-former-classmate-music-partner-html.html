Phylis Leina: Reuben Kigame Pens Emotional Eulogy to Former Classmate, Music Partner

Publish date: 2024-02-04

- Phyllis Leina Nemayan was classmates with Reuben Kigame at Thika School of the Blind

- She died on Thursday, April 1, at night; however, the musician did not disclose the cause her death

- Kigame had recorded her a new song five days ago and was yet to launch it

Kenyan gospel musician Reuben Kigame has penned a very emotional eulogy to his former high school classmate, who died on Thursday, April 1, at night.

The musician stated that the late Phyllis Leina Nemayan was his classmate from Form One to Form Six at Thika School for the Blind.

Kigame eulogised Nemayan as a great prayer warrior whose prayers kept him going, adding that the deceased used to help him a lot in his journey of faith.

"No longer with us! Hard to believe! I just got her doctorate last year, which my assistant and I assisted with work-wise. My classmate from form 1 to 6 at Thika High School for the Blind. A member of my first music group as a new Christian called ‘The Ambassadors," he stated.

He also remembered how close they were with Nemanyan's family saying her family used to travel all the way from Machakos to his home in Eldoret.

The celebrated musician further disclosed that they worshipped together and became music partners. He had recorded her new song and shared it with her before she passed on.

Kigame said when he shared the new song waiting to be launched, the deceased was very happy and lauded him for his sweet voice.

''Helped me a lot in my journey of faith. A great prayer warrior whose prayers have kept me going. . I had just finished recording her song, “Nifike Salama”, at our studios. When I sent her the sample mix five days ago, this is what she texted on Telegram: “Wa! Wa! Unbelievable! Amazingly melodious thanks for the job well done. God bless your ministry," he wrote .

Kigame, who is also a writer said Nemayan had requested him to join his long-serving choir, the Sifa Voices.

However, the musicians dint not disclosed the cause of death of his friend.
