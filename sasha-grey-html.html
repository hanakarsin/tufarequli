Sasha Grey - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-16

Retired adult film actress who made her mainstream film debut in The Girlfriend Experience and Smash Cut. She has published four books and she regularly performs as a DJ. 

Before Fame

She attended four high schools before briefly studying at Sacramento City College. She later dropped out.

Trivia

She had a recurring role on the 2010 season of the hit HBO series Entourage. She's appeared in music videos for such artists as Eminem, The Smashing Pumpkins and The Roots. 

Family Life

She was engaged to director Ian Cinnamon in 2009. The couple eventually separated and she got a restraining order against Ian.

Associated With

She co-starred in the independent film Open Windows with Elijah Wood in 2012.
