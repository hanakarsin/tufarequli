Pregnant Comedian, Maraji Reveals Father Of Her Unborn Child, Shares Photo

Publish date: 2024-03-13

Instagram sensation, Gloria Oloruntobi better known by her stage name, Maraji has revealed she’s married to a Ghanaian.

Naija News understands Maraji resurfaced on social media some couple of days ago with a baby bump.

The comedian who has been absent on social media for quite some time recently uploaded a video of her dancing with her baby bump clearly visible.

In a new video, however, Maraji talked about her marriage, noting that she did not have a big wedding.

According to the 24-year-old comedian, the wedding took place in her sitting room with her in-laws and her family present to witness the wedding.

Meanwhile, the son of the General Overseer of Redeemed Christian Church of Good (RCCG), Pastor Leke Adeboye and his wife, Pastor Titilope Adeboye marked their 10th wedding anniversary today, December 28, 2021.

Naija News understands that the couple got married in 2011.

While celebrating the special day, Leke urged members to remember their marriage in  their prayers as they do.

Taking to his social media pages to celebrate this day, he said, ‘’Thank you for your 10th year wedding anniversary, greetings and blessings, I pray your own years will be greater and your light brighter in JESUS name. Please do remember our marriage in your prayers always as we remember you.”
