The 10 best action series of all time

Publish date: 2024-06-08

Action series are some of the most-watched genres. The best action series of all time feature little-known actors, who thanks to their roles in the shows, blew up and became major stars just out of the single shows they did. Michael Scofield and Kiefer Sutherland are some of the actors whose names are associated with some of the biggest action and drama series on TV.

The best action TV series will have the viewer forget about everything that is happening and concentrate on the drama. Series producers do not just focus on the action, but also the conversations, distinct traits on main characters, and how good series regulars execute their roles.

Many of the best action series have remained on TV for years because fans are impressed by what the producers and directors do and how interesting the plot gets to be with every new season. Here are the top 10 series that you should consider watching:
