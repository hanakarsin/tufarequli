Manuel Turizo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Colombian reggaeton recording artist who first became widely recognized for his breakout single "Una Lady Como Tu." He released his first studio album ADN in 2019. He's also known for collaborations such as "Vaina Loca" with Ozuna and "Déjala que Vuelva" with Piso 21.

Before Fame

He began taking voice lessons at a young age. He wanted to be a veterinarian early in his life. 

Trivia

He appeared on the popular Netflix original series La Reina del Flow and he also sings the show's theme song, "Quiero Ser un Cantante." He won Best New Latin Artist at the iHeartRadio Music Awards in 2019.

Family Life

He has a brother named Julian who has performed with him. His father was also a musician. He began dating Instagram model Joselina Sorza. He has a pet dog named Arya.

Associated With

He collaborated with Mau y Ricky and Camilo on the 2018 hit song "Desconocidos."
