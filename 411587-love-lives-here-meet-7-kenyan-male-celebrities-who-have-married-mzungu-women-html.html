Love Lives Here: Meet 7 Kenyan Male Celebrities Who Have Married Mzungu Women

Publish date: 2024-05-07

- They say love has no boundaries, and we all agree to that

- Several Kenyan celebrities are thriving in interracial marriages, and Dj Moz, Jeff Okell aka Ninja, are some of them

-Dj Moz, whose real name is Moses Kimathi Mathenge, and his wife Deborah have been married for 12 years and blessed with three children

Kenyan men have been criticised several times for being unromantic, stingy and not caring, but for our mzungu sisters from developed countries, these men are a treasure.

Most Kenyan women prefer West African men, especially Nigerians, to their own.

We have several Kenyan celebrities who married white (mzungu) women, and below is a list of seven.

1. Jeff Koinange

The Citizen TV and Hot 96 presenter is married to the beautiful Shaila Koinange. She is a Kenyan of Asian origin.

The couple struggled to have a baby for nine years after getting married and even tried countless methods to conceive. They later conceived their only child, Jamal Mbiyu Koinange.

In his book Through My African Eyes, Jeff narrated how they struggled before his wife conceived.

"We had practically given up until Jimmy Monclus Maleketa, a half-Spanish, half Congolese friend we met in Lagos suggested we try invitro-fertilization (IVF) at a renowned fertility clinic in Barcelona, Spain," Jeff wrote.

2. Albert Wanyonyi aka Bukusu darling

The bodaboda rider is married to a mzungu missionary Carey Joy.

Carey met her Bukusu bae in 2018, the first time she came to Kenya, and she fell in love with him.

They tied the knot in late 2018, a few months after they met, and are blessed with two children.

3. Dj Moz

Moses Kimathi Mathenge aka Dj Moz and his wife Deborah are living happily. They are blessed with three adorable kids, two daughters and a son.

The couple tied the knot many moons ago, and in October 2020, they celebrated their 12th marriage anniversary.

12 years. God has been really good to us. Never thought I would be here blessed with an amazing wife and 3 lovely kids. God is faithful and He hooked me up real good. You can trust Him. I’m a witness of His goodness," DJ Moz posted on their anniversary.

4. Jeff Okello aka Ninja

The Mother-in-law actor married the love of his life, Katja Maria Huhta, in 2018. They are blessed with two adorable biracial kids.

The couple lives abroad.

Ninja and his mzungu wife celebrated their third wedding anniversary in January this year.

5. Octopizzo

The Kibra born and bred artiste is a father of three kids with different women. Octopizzo's current wife is a mzungu, and they have a cute daughter together named Zara.

6. Mukhisa Kituyi

The 2022 presidential seat hopeful is married to a Norwegian wife, Ling Kituyi.

The couple had four children, but one died in 2015, aged 30.

7. The late Ken Okoth

The most loved member of parliament for Kibra constituency was married to a mzungu woman, Monica.

The couple had no children by the time Ken succumbed to cancer in July 2019.

Speaking at the memorial service for her husband at Starehe Boys Centre in August 2019, Monica revealed that she and Ken had known each other for 21 years.

"I met Ken Okoth at the university. He was the only African student in our Austrian university and he became our student leader."
