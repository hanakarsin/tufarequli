Diamond Platnumz Nominated for BET 2021 Awards International Act Category

Publish date: 2024-05-02

- Diamond Platnumz has been nominated for this year's BET Awards

- The Gere singer will face off with other music GOATS like Burna Boy, Wizkid and Headie from the UK to bag the best artiste in the International Act Category Award

- He has been consistent in releasing hit songs and doing collaborations with global artistes solidifying his fan-base all around the world

Tanzanian singer Diamond Platnumz keeps solidifying his name as one of the greatest musicians in Africa, and he has just been nominated for the BET Awards, International Act category.

The 31-year-old will be facing off with other renowned artistes like Nigeria's Burna Boy, who has in the past bagged the BET Award, Wizkid, Brazil's Emicida, Young T & Bugsey from the UK, among others.

This is what Tanzanian journalist Millard Ayo shared on his Facebook page about Diamond's nomination:

"Bongo Flavour Star Diamond Platnumz has been nominated on the best artistes on the International Act category for the BET 2021 awards. Others nominated in the same category are Burna Boy, Wizkid, Headie, Emicida, Aya Nakamura, Youssoupha and Young T & Bugsey wa UK."

However, the 2021 awards will be held on June 28.

Not the first time

This is not the first time Diamond is getting nominated for the award, but he is yet to win. His protege Rayvanny won a BET Award in 2017 for the viewer's choice Best New International Act category, and he said it was all Diamond's efforts and faith in him that made him win the award.
