Nkechi Blessing Opens Up On How She Got Funds to Complete Her New House

Publish date: 2024-02-25

Nollywood actress, Nkechi Blessing has opened up on how she was able to complete her house in 2021.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that the actress had unveiled her new house and thanked God for the success in completing it because it was no easy feat for her.

In a new post on Instagram, Nkechi has opened up on how she was able to get money to finish up her new home.

She stated that she had to sell her Range Rover to complete the house.

The actress maintained that it was the best decision she took last year as a car is never an achievement but a necessity.

She wrote: “The best decision I made in 2021 was selling my Range Rover to complete this beauty behind me. Tnk God for the space bus I bought for my mum. Shey landlady for Dey trek by now, It’s 2022 Try set your priorities right.

“A car is never an achievement but a necessity,a means of transporting you from one place to another. This is my own opinion and I did not call for a debate..

“Choose whatever works for you pls..Just saying respectfully.”
