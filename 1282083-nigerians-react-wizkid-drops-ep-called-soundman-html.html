Nigerians react as Wizkid drops EP called Soundman

Publish date: 2024-04-06

Nigerian singer, Ayodeji Balogun, better known as Wizkid, is no doubt one of the top musicians in the country and fans are constantly waiting for him to as much as cough on social media so they can enjoy his talent.

On December 6, 2019, the Baba Nla crooner released an extended playlist called Soundman and Nigerians on social media cannot seem to get enough of it.

Several fans and haters of Wizkid took to Twitter to share their opinion on the tracks on the Soundman EP. Some marvelled at how the music star released a major body of work that has got people talking despite not publicising it beforehand.

@iamOko said: “Wizkid is so confident in his ability & his craft that he didn’t need to hype or make too much noise but dropped a whole project “like a thief at night” and he knows we’ll all love #SoundManEP. That’s what happens when you’ve worked hard & paid your dues. Legend. Starboy.”

Chidi Okereke: “Wizkid really woke up this morning and told his team “drop that tape”. No prior announcement. No publicity. Drop the dam*n tape. Bruh, what a focken Lion ”

Alhaji Royz tweeted: “No other Nigerian artiste can take the daring steps that Wizkid takes. He is really confident that he is the only star boy.

Man disappears whenever he wants, comes back whenever he wants, drops tracks whenever he wants, drops EP whenever he wants. A focken king!”

Danny Walter wrote: “Wizkid just pulled an Adele x Beyoncé stunt on us.. Releasing a Musical project When it’s least expected. Only someone very confident of their Talent and sound can pull that through! #soundman”.

There were however some negative comments from people who felt Wizkid released the EP to counter Davido's recently dropped album.

Jordan tweeted: "The fear of DAVIDO'S A GOOD TIME ALBUM is the reason wizkid is releasing an EP instead of MIL wizkid just thank your stars you didn't release that your Made In Thrash album caused we'd have talked the album back to ojuelegba where it came from. Btw your stupid fans started it."
