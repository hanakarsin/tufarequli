Oluwafunsho, Mercy Aigbe Co-wife Spills It All, Says She Introduced Actress To Her Husband

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Oluwafunsho Asiwaju, the first wife of Mercy Aigbe’s current husband Kazim “Adekaz” Adeoti has made very shocking allegations against her co-wife.

Mercy had said earlier that she and her co-wife were not friends and her husband was free to marry multiple wives because he is a Muslim.

Responding to Mercy claims and her husband’s recent interview, Asiwaju insisted via Instagram that she and Mercy were friends as she was the one who introduced her to their husband. She claimed that Mercy has been sleeping with her husband since her son now 11 was 5-months- old.

“You were a constant friend of mine back then. I brought you close as a friend because I liked you, but you repaid me by sleeping with my husband Kazim. “

“You were a problem in my marriage for years.”

She stated further that she never accepted a second wife in her union with her husband and explained that she plans on divorcing him soon.

“Contrary to what Mr Adeoti said, I did not and will never settle to have a second wife in my marriage to him.”
