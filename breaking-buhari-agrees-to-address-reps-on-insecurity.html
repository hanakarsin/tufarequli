Buhari Agrees To Address Reps On Insecurity

Publish date: 0001-01-01

President Muhammadu Buhari on Wednesday agreed to address the House of Representatives over the security challenges rocking the nation.

Naija News reports that this was made known by the Speaker of the House, Femi Gbajabiamila, after a meeting with the president.

Speaking to State House correspondents, Gbajabiamila said the date of the address would soon be made public.

Recall that the House of Reps on Tuesday resolved to invite Buhari to address lawmakers on efforts being made to address the security challenges in parts of the country following the massacre of 43 rice farmers in Borno.
