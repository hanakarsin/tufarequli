Hailee Steinfeld - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-13

Became an Oscar nominee at age fourteen for her role in the 2010 film True Grit. In 2015, she appeared in Pitch Perfect 2 and later starred in The Edge of Seventeen and Bumblebee. Also a singer, she released her debut EP Haiz, featuring the single "Love Myself," in 2015. 

Before Fame

She began acting in short films at the age of eight. She was homeschooled from age 11 through high school. 

Trivia

In 2013, she starred as Juliet Capulet in Romeo and Juliet and played Petra in Ender's Game. She also provided her voice for the 2018 animated film Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse.

Family Life

Her parents are Cheri Steinfeld and Peter Steinfeld and she has an older brother named Griffin. She began dating quarterback Josh Allen in 2023. She previously dated Niall Horan in 2018 and dated Cameron Smoller in 2017. 

Associated With

She appeared in Taylor Swift's music video for "Bad Blood" in 2015.
