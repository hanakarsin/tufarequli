Sierra Kay - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-26

Famous as the frontwoman of the Florida rock band Versa, she released her first studio album with the group, Fixed at Zero, in 2010. Her band's singles include "Past Praying For," "Lost Tree," and "Neon."

Before Fame

After dropping out of her performing arts high school, she formed Versa and released a debut EP titled Cities Built on Sand.

Trivia

A model as well as a musician, she was featured in advertisements for Payless shoes.

Family Life

Born Sierra Kay Kusterbeck, she grew up in St. Petersburg, Florida.

Associated With

She is featured on British hip hop artist Professor Green's hit 2012 single "Avalon."
