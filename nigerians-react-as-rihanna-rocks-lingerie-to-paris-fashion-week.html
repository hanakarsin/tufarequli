Nigerians React As Rihanna Rocks Lingerie To Paris Fashion Week

Publish date: 2024-02-24

Nigerian netizens have expressed their outright disapproval over the outfit wore by American singer and billionaire, Rihanna, 34, at the  ongoing Paris Fashion Week which started on Monday, February 28, 2022.

Naija News reports that the 34 year old popstar arrived the Dior show of Paris Fashion Week wearing a tiny black thong and lacy bra underneath a totally sheer skirt pulled up over her bump.

She also accessorized with thigh-high black patent leather boots and a matching coat, flaunting baby bump with pride as the eyes of all the photographers and the people present were fixed on her only.

The makeup mogul who have been baring her bump nearly every day since announcing that she is pregnant with her first child, took nude maternity wear up another level on Tuesday when she attended the Dior show at Paris Fashion Week.

However, Nigerian via Instagram expressed their displeasure, whilst asking the pop star to do better.

Reacting to the outfit, one nechelsea wrote; ”She’s not trying to avoid anything, just enjoying her pregnancy journey”

One enejovictor wrote; ”What happened to decency and chivalry. Is this fashion again? Wrong on so many levels.”

One fatiregunoluwakemi wrote; ‘She has money she is wearing nonsense this life’

One Tinuke wrote “See your stupid caption, stole the show, if na tiwa savage dress like this or any Nigerian celebrity una caption go be she dressed naked to a show, see ba, you bloggers are one of the problems we have in this country, na una go dey shout racism up and down but yet once it’s a white thing you will glorify it, if na your own fellow black, na to condemn am… ori gbogbo yin ti daru”

One Temidayo wrote “So because she is pregnant now, she won’t wear cloth again? Smh, she looks stupid. What’s wrong with this generation of women?”
